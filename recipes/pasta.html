Simple Creamy Pasta

This easy pasta recipe is creamy, cheesy, and super comforting.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pasta (penne or macaroni)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • Grated cheese
  • Salt, pepper, oregano, chili flakes

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water for 8–10 minutes
  2. Drain water and keep pasta aside
  3. In a pan, melt butter and add milk/cream
  4. Add pasta, cheese, and spices
  5. Mix well until creamy and hot — done!
